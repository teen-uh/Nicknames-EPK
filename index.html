“Funky rock disco pop with a hip hop twang. Five big booms.”
- Angie, Girl at Party

With his third EP, You Don’t Wanna Miss This, LA-based artist & producer, Nicknames, delivers a fresh summer sound that is guaranteed to get you moving. The self-produced project contains 6 tracks with falsetto hooks that will tickle your ears, basslines that are already stuck in your head, and production that you will definitely not want to miss…this.
